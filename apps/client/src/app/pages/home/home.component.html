NearestStars.com was created in 2018 to serve the purpose of both educating the public about our Solar neighborhood, as well as be a center for the latest research on all sorts of stars, from huge blue giants, to freezing rogue planets lurking in between the stars.

In its current state, the site is still under construction to arrive at our primary goal at listing the 100 nearest star systems to the Sun, so content may change significantly.

Stay tuned, and thanks for visiting the site!
